Terms & Conditions — Biometric Login

1. Consent: By enabling biometric login, you consent to using your fingerprint (or Face ID on supported devices) to authenticate your identity within Fleet Manager.

2. Security: Your biometric data is processed entirely on your device by the operating system. Fleet Manager never stores, transmits, or has access to your biometric data.

3. Token: A secure cryptographic token is stored locally on your device to verify your identity with the server. This token does not contain any biometric data.

4. Revocation: You may disable biometric login at any time from your Profile page. Clearing app data or changing your device fingerprints will automatically invalidate the token.

5. Responsibility: Any person whose fingerprint is enrolled on this device may be able to authenticate as you. Ensure your device security is maintained.

6. Fallback: Your password login remains active at all times as a fallback authentication method.
